About Harini Murugan

I'm a student at Victoria University of Wellington currently studying Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). I have a strong interest in data visualization. In order to develop the visualization, I used tools like D3.js, Tailwind CSS, and JavaScript to turn raw data into meaningful tales..

In this project, I developed a visualization showcasing the Top 10 Lakes by Pollution Level based on selected pollution indicators. The visualization includes dynamic charts, color gradients, and responsive tooltips to help users explore pollution levels clearly and interactively.
